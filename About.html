ABOUT US

ABOUT THE
Twisted lines apparel

Twisted Lines was created because the owners, who are Professional Sky Divers, struggled finding a manufacture to design and produce high quality clothing for jumping into shows and events. After years of searching for quality material, testing, and thousands of dollars of free samples to gain product feedback, we are now the ONLY Manufactured cut and sew in the state of Texas. We have created a mentality of excellence. 80% of our product sales have consistently been repeat business. If you are looking for a one stop shop, a place where your idea, brand, and companies’ future marketing is important, then look no further, Twisted Lines Apparel.
